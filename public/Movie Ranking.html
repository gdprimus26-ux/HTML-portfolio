The Best Movies According to Ghinelle

My Top 3 movies of all time.

The Lord of the Rings

This movie has every element you can think of. From action, adventure to romance and overall just meets my every expectations.

The Notebook

I love a good love story and this one just takes the whole cake. Call me a hopeless romantic I don't care.It's timeless and magical and gives me hope and I love that.

Big Fish

Where do I begin? I saw this movie as a child and it was just so big to me, pun intended. It was just so magical and positive and just gave me a sense of hope. It's just that sometimes you need to be reminded that you can push through things and there is light at the end of the tunnel and life is so beautiful so many different people you meet and how they can impact your life and also the impact you can have on their lives as well.
